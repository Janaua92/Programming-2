Panzenella Salad

A rustic sald made with stale crusty bread cubes, mixed with cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion and basil, dressed with olive oil and red wine vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 200g Stale Crusty Bread, Cut Into Cubes
  • 250g Cherry Tomatoes, Halved
  • 1 Cucumber, Diced
  • 1/2 Red Onion, Thinly Sliced
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Bread - Lightly toast bread cubes or soak them in water for 1-2 minutes, then squeeze out excess moisture.
  2. Combine Ingredients - In a large bowl, mix bread, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and basil.
  3. Dress the Salad - Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar. Season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Let it Sit - Allow flavors to blend for 10-15 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
