Margherita Pizza

A classic Neapolitan pizza featuring a thin, crispy crust with tangy tomato sauce, frash mozzarella, fragrant basil, and a dizzle of olive oil.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 500g All-purpose Flour
  • 325ml Warm Water
  • 10g Salt
  • 3g Dry Yeast or 1g Fresh Yeast
  • 10ml Olive Oil

Toppings

  • 300g Canned San Marzano Tomatoes, blended
  • 250g Fresh Mozzarella Sliced
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough - Mix flour, salt, yeast, water, and olive oil. Knead for 10 minutes and let rise for 2 hours.
  2. Prepare the Sauce - Blend canned tomatoes with a pinch of salt.
  3. Preheat the Oven - Heat to 250Â°C. If using a pizza stone, preheat it too.
  4. Shape the Dough - Roll out a thin 12-inch circle.
  5. Assemble the Pizza - Spread tomato sauce, add mozzarella slices and basil, drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake - Bake for 7-10 minutes until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve - Slice and drizzle extra olive oil.
