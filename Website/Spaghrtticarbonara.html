Spaghetti Carbonara

A rich and creamy classic Italian pasta dish that combines silky cheese sauce with crisp pancetta and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 350g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 50g Pecorino Cheese
  • 50g Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta - Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Save ½ cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. Mix the sauce - Whisk eggs, Parmesan, Pecorino, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Cook the pancetta - Melt butter in a pan, sauté garlic, add pancetta, and cook until crispy.
  4. Combine everything - Add drained pasta to the pan, remove from heat, stir in egg mixture, and toss quickly. Add pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  5. Serve - Top with extra cheese and black pepper.
