Rustic Salad

A more traditional chopped salad creating the perfect mix of sweet and savoury. Featuring crisp greens, fresh vegtables, cured meats, and cheese.

Ingredients

Salad Base

  • 4 Cups Mixed Greens (Romaine, Arugula, or Radicchio)
  • 1 Cucumber, Diced
  • 200g Cherry Tomatoes, Halved
  • 1/2 Red Onion, Thinly Sliced
  • 1/2 Cup Black or Green Olives, Sliced

Meats and Cheese

  • 100g Salami, Sliced
  • 100g Prosciutto, Sliced
  • 100g Mozzarella, Cubed
  • 100g Parmesan, Shaved

Dressing

  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Red Wine Vinigar
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients - Chop all vegetables, meats, and cheese into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Make the Dressing - Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper.
  3. Assemble the Salad - Toss greens, vegetables, meats, and cheese in a large bowl.
  4. Dress & Serve - Drizzle with dressing, mix well, and enjoy fresh!
