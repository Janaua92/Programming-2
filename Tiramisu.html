Tiramisu

A rich and creamy dessert made with layers of coffee soaked ladyfingers and sweet mascarpone cream, finished with a dusting of cocoa powder.

Ingredients

Cream

  • 250g Mascarpone Cheese
  • 2 Eggs Yolks
  • 50g Suger
  • 200ml Heavy Cream, Whipped
  • 1 tbs Vanilla Extract

Layers

  • 200g Ladyfingers
  • 200ml Strong Coffee, Cooled
  • 2 tbs Coffee Liqueur

Toppings

  • Cocoa Powder, for Dusting
  • Chocolate Shavings, for Garnish

Instructions

  1. Make the Cream - Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy. Mix in mascarpone and vanilla, then fold in whipped cream.
  2. Prepare the Ladyfingers - Dip ladyfingers quickly in coffee (with liqueur if using).
  3. Assemble - Layer half the ladyfingers in a dish, spread half the mascarpone cream on top. Repeat with another layer.
  4. Chill - Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (best overnight).
  5. Serve - Dust with cocoa powder, add chocolate shavings if desired, and enjoy!
