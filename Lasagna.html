Classic Italian Lasagna

Classic Italian Lasagna with a creamy béchamel sauce and classic defined layers.

Ingredients

Meet Sauce

  • 500g Ground Beef (or Mix of Beef and Pork)
  • 1 Small Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 400g Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 100ml Red Wine (Optional)
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Salt and Black Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil

Béchamel Sauce

  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Flour
  • 500ml Milk
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • Salt

Other Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Sheets
  • 200g Mozzarella Cheese, Shredded
  • 200g Parmesan Cheese, Grated

Instructions

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce - Sauté onion and garlic in oil. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, wine (optional), oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the Béchamel Sauce - Melt butter, stir in flour, cook for 1 minute. Slowly add milk, stirring until thick. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna - Layer meat sauce, lasagna sheets, béchamel, and cheese in a baking dish. Repeat until full, finishing with béchamel and cheese on top.
  4. Bake - Cover with foil and bake at 180°C for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes until golden.
  5. Serve - Let rest 10 minutes and slice.
