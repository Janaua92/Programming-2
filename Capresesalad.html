Caprese Salad

Classic italian tricolore salad made with perfectly ripe tomatoes, mozzarella, and freshed basil, drizzled with olive oli and seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Ripe Tomatoes, Sliced
  • 250g Fresh Mozzarella, Sliced
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Balsamic Glaze
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Slice the Ingredients - Cut tomatoes and mozzarella into even slices.
  2. Assemble the Salad - Arrange tomato, mozzarella, and basil leaves in alternating layers on a plate.
  3. Drizzle & Season - Drizzle with olive oil (and balsamic glaze, if using). Sprinkle with salt and black pepper.
  4. Combine everything - Add drained pasta to the pan, remove from heat, stir in egg mixture, and toss quickly. Add pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  5. Serve - Enjoy fresh as a side or appetizer!
