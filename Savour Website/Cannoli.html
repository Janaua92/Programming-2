Cannoli

Sicilian delicacy with crispy fried pastry shells filled with sweet ricotta cream, flavoured with chocolate chips, candied fruit, or pistachios.

Ingredients

Shell

  • 250g All-purpose Flour
  • 30g Sugar
  • 30g Butter, Melted
  • 1 Egg
  • 50ml Marsala Wine
  • 1 tsp Cocoa Powder
  • 1 Pinch Salt
  • Vegetable Oil, For Frying

Filling

  • 400g Ricotta Cheese
  • 100g Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 50g Chocolate Chips
  • 50g Candied Fruit

Garnish

  • Powdered Sugar
  • Pistachios, Chopped

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough - Mix flour, sugar, cocoa (if using), butter, egg, and wine into a smooth dough. Wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Shape & Fry Shells - Roll dough thin, cut circles, wrap around metal cannoli tubes, and seal. Fry in hot oil (180°C/350°F) until golden. Drain and cool.
  3. Make the Filling - Mix ricotta, powdered sugar, vanilla, and chocolate chips until smooth.
  4. Fill the Cannoli - Pipe ricotta cream into cooled shells.
  5. Garnish & Serve - Dip ends in pistachios, chocolate, or candied fruit. Dust with powdered sugar and enjoy!
