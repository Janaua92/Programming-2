Panna Cotta

A smooth, creamy custard like desert, often topped with barry compote or caramel sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500ml Heavy Cream
  • 100ml Milk
  • 80g Suger
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tsp Gelatin Powder
  • 3 tbsp Cold Water
  • Berry Compote (Strawberries, Raspberris, Blueberries with Suger)
  • Caramel Sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare Gelatin - Sprinkle gelatin over cold water, let sit for 5 minutes to bloom.
  2. Heat Cream Mixture - In a saucepan, heat cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla over medium heat until warm (donâ€™t boil).
  3. Dissolve Gelatin - Remove from heat, stir in bloomed gelatin until fully dissolved.
  4. Pour & Chill - Pour into ramekins or glasses. Refrigerate for 4-6 hours until set.
  5. Serve - Top with berry compote or caramel sauce, and enjoy!
